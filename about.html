About Dollah Cafe

Dollah Café began its journey in 2014 as a humble drink stall at a food court in Belawai. Initially focused on quenching thirst with simple beverages, the stall quickly gained a loyal following for its friendly service and quality offerings.

Over time, the family behind the stall decided to expand their menu and transform the small business into a full-fledged café. Today, Dollah Café stands proudly next to the owner’s house, offering not just drinks but a variety of hearty dishes such as nasi goreng, mee goreng, and more.

Despite its growth, the café remains a family-operated business, with every aspect—from cooking to serving—managed by family members. This personal touch has made Dollah Café a warm and welcoming spot for locals and visitors alike.

Our Signature Creation

The café is best known for its signature dessert, ABC Tinggi or ABC Viral, a towering shaved ice creation that has captured the hearts (and social media feeds) of many. While the café’s vibrant dessert has gained viral fame, Dollah Café is also cherished as a community space where people can lepak, enjoying good food, great company, and the relaxed atmosphere of a family-run business.

Our Vision

To become a beloved local destination where people of all ages gather to enjoy authentic flavors, fostering a sense of community and celebrating Malaysia’s rich culinary heritage.

Our Mission

Our mission is to preserve Malaysia's rich food culture by serving authentic dishes with quality ingredients, offering a warm dining experience, supporting our community, and prioritizing sustainability.
